Frequently asked questions

If you still have questions after this, you can ask directly in WhatsApp when you send your details.

I don’t know if my resume is “good” or not. Which package should I choose? ▼

If you are not sure whether your resume is suitable for your target role, start with the Resume Match Audit. You’ll see exactly where your resume is strong, where it is weak, and whether you need a full ATS rewrite. If you already know your resume is weak or outdated, choose ATS Resume Rewrite or the Career Pack.

Can I use this if I have no experience or I’m a fresh graduate? ▼

Yes. Many clients are fresh graduates or people with internships, part-time and project experience. The focus is on how to position what you already have to match the job requirements, not on “faking” experience.

Is this only for Malaysian jobs? ▼

The system is designed with Malaysian job postings, HR practices and ATS patterns in mind. However, many principles still apply for international roles. If you are targeting overseas positions, mention it clearly in the WhatsApp form.

What if the result is still not what I want? ▼

If, after using your included requests (according to your package), your resume is still completely misaligned with your target role, I will advise you on realistic next steps. The goal is to be transparent about what your profile can and cannot support.

Will you apply for jobs on my behalf? ▼

No. I do not submit applications or contact recruiters on your behalf. I help you with your documents (resume, match score, cover letters, mock interview questions) so you can apply with a stronger profile.
