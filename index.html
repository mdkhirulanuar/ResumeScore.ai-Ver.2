How CareerAlign works

The process is guided and simple. You don’t have to “know how to write a resume”.

1

Share your resume & target job

Upload your current resume and job description (or job link). Tell me your target role and any special situation such as fresh graduate, retrenched or career switch. One main role per report for maximum precision.

2

AI-assisted analysis & scoring

I analyse your resume against the job requirements, score the alignment, highlight missing keywords / experience and check ATS-readiness. You get a structured, consultant-style view of your strengths and gaps.

3

Receive your tailored documents

Based on your chosen package, you receive your match score report, ATS-optimised resume, tailored cover letter and mock interview questions for your role. You apply with documents that are aligned to your target job.
